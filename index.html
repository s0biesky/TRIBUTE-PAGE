Marie Curie - 1920

Here's a short timeline of Marie Curie's life:

  • 1867 - Born in Warsaw, Poland
  • 1891 - Followed her elder sister to study in Paris, where she earned her higher degrees and conducted her subsequent scientific work
  • 1895 - Married the French physicist Pierre Curie
  • 1903 - Wins the Nobel Prize in Physics and the Davy Medal
  • 1904 - Wins the Matteucci Medal
  • 1907 - Wins the Actonian Prize
  • 1909 - Wins the Elliott Cresson medal
  • 1910 - Wins the Albert Medal
  • 1911 - Wins the Nobel Prize in Chemistry
  • 1921 - Wins the Willard Gibbs Award and the John Scott Medal
  • 1931 - Wins the Cameron Prize for Therapeutics of the University of Edinburgh
  • 1934 - Dies at the age of 66
